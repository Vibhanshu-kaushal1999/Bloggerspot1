The word “blog,” a contraction of “web log,” is an informational web page that consists of discrete units called “posts.” The term “log” comes from the history of nautical navigation and originally referred to a record of chronological events kept by a ship’s crew (Rettberg 30). Like a ship’s log, blogs on the web tend to maintain an ordered chronology, although they often unfold in reverse order. Blog posts are often shorter and less formal than other types of writing.
